Mission

We build technology that eliminates waste.

Wasted code. Wasted processes. Wasted resources. Wasted potential. Swrthy delivers solutions worthy of the people and institutions they serve.

Swrthy is a Florida-based technology solutions company that partners with independent businesses and government entities to reduce technical debt, remove operational bottlenecks, and deploy purposeful software that creates measurable impact. We build products and platforms — like Srplus, our food rescue technology — that connect surplus to need, complexity to clarity, and capability to those who lack it.

Vision — A world where no resource goes wasted — not food, not technology, not human potential — because the right systems exist to connect them.

Core Beliefs

  • 01 Worthy Over Cheap

    Every system Swrthy delivers must be worthy of the institution it serves and the people it affects. Quality is the baseline.

  • 02 Stewardship Is Strategy

    Responsible use of resources — financial, technical, and human — is not just ethical. Waste is a liability. Stewardship is a competitive advantage.

  • 03 Technical Debt Is a Justice Issue

    When government systems are broken, the people who suffer most are those with the fewest options. We treat legacy modernization as a community service.

  • 04 Safety Is Non-Negotiable

    We build secure systems by default — no corner-cutting on authentication, data handling, access control, or compliance.

  • 05 Profitability Enables Purpose

    Profit funds our ability to build more products like Srplus and serve clients who couldn't otherwise afford enterprise-grade solutions.

  • 06 Small Teams, Big Systems

    A small, technically elite team operating with discipline and clarity can outperform a large, bureaucratic firm on every meaningful metric.

  • 07 Transparency Builds Contracts

    We document everything. We communicate clearly. We never oversell scope or hide technical limitations. Trust is the contract before the contract.

  • 08 Products Outlast Projects

    Wherever possible, we build reusable, scalable software that serves multiple clients — not one-off builds that die with the engagement.

  • 09 The Gift Must Flow

    Technology — like living water — is most powerful when it moves, reaches new places, and gives life to communities that have been without it. John 4:10

  • 10 Worthy Means Accountable

    We stand behind our work. If a Swrthy system fails, we show up. Accountability separates a worthy vendor from one that just survived procurement.
